Who we are

Climate Fintech for a Resilient Future

mistEO is a Climate Fintech company dedicated to helping businesses, governments, and communities adapt to climate change. By integrating satellite data, IoT, weather stations, and AI-driven models, mistEO delivers actionable climate intelligence. Their tools support operational risk management, insurance innovation, and financial resilience in climate-sensitive sectors like agriculture, banking, and energy. Focused on adaptation-not just mitigation-mistEO empowers decision-makers with the tools they need to navigate today’s climate risks and build a resilient future.

Our Climate Strategies

All are urgent One demands action right now.

Climate action has three pillars, but adaptation is where mistEO focuses - tackling the risks that businesses, communities, and governments face today.

Climate Mitigation

Efforts aimed at stopping or reducing the emission of greenhouse gases, into the atmosphere and slow down the pace of climate change.

Decarbonisation

Removal of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases that have already been emitted into the atmosphere to bring the planet back to natural levels.

Climate Adaptation

Encompasses strategies and measures taken to address the impacts of climate change that are already occurring or expected to occur. It involves adjusting to changing climate conditions and enhancing resilience.
